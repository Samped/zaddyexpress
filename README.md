## ZaddyExpress

ZaddyExpress is a fast, reliable, and user-friendly delivery application built to connect users with nearby dispatch riders instantly.
With peer-to-peer pricing, real-time tracking, and seamless communication, ZaddyExpress makes sending and receiving packages simple and efficient.

## Features
#For Users

Instant Rider Matching: Connect with the closest dispatch rider automatically.

Real-Time Tracking: Monitor delivery progress live from pickup to drop-off.

Peer-to-Peer Pricing: Bid your preferred fare, with a base minimum price.

Multiple Pickup & Drop-off: Add several locations for multi-stop deliveries.

In-App Chat & Call: Communicate instantly with riders.

Secure Payments: Pay in-app via card or bank transfer after delivery.

Ratings & Feedback: Rate riders and share your delivery experience.

#or Riders

Rider Dashboard: View earnings, completed trips, and withdrawals easily.

KYC Verification: Secure onboarding with NIN and face recognition.

Online/Offline Mode: Toggle availability for receiving delivery requests.

Order Notifications: Accept or ignore requests based on your schedule.

Route Navigation: Get pickup and drop-off directions seamlessly.

## Admin Dashboard

Real-time analytics on users and riders

Insights into peak hours, demand hotspots, and delivery performance

Monitoring of active, completed, and canceled deliveries
